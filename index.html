About Me

Hi there! I’m Azeez, a nature-loving researcher and innovator passionate about sustainable development in Africa. My journey into Collective Intelligence began when I saw how communities could solve complex challenges—like sustainable farming—by pooling their knowledge. With a Master’s in Collective Intelligence from UM6P, I’ve dedicated my career to harnessing CI for social impact, from founding CrowdSmarter to modeling various forms of communication using CI principles. I’m currently an Outreach Officer for the Africa Initiative at UM6P, Morocco, sowing seeds of change across the continent, and I’m actively exploring PhD opportunities to deepen my impact in CI and sustainability.

My CI Projects

Explore my mini projects empowering Collective Intelligence

EquiVoice

EquiVoice ensures your conversational intelligence reflects fair discussions—catching when one voice dominates, others fade, or groupthink stalls ideas. Still in development yet experimentally functional, it also works with messy transcripts, offering a clear summary, vivid charts, key words, and teamwork insights.

View Project

Group Decision Facilitator

A lightweight tool to facilitate group decision-making using collective input. Perfect for small teams or communities to align on choices effectively. Explore group dynamics, test voting strategies, or support real-world decisions in communities and teams.

View Project

CICFO

Collective Intelligence for Cluster Farming Optimization (CICFO) empowers farming clusters to optimize scarce resources (water, fertilizers, labor) using collective intelligence. Together, we promote sustainability and resilience against climate and market challenges.
